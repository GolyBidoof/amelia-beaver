Let's talk about idolizing people: or, why not to do it!

Putting someone on a pedestal is the number one barrier keeping me from being intimate with someone else.

Sometimes, when you idolize a person, you both consider yourselves to be really cool people, and you both like one another.

You instantly begin forming a parasocial relationship with that person in your head, and maybe even an asexual squish with them?

And then you never see a person for who they really are. You tread carefully around them.

"What if I say something stupid and I mess it all up?"

You've just messed up by not saying something stupid and letting them see you as a person.
