One of my traits I struggle to make terms with is the inability to be happy with the current situation, no matter what it is.

It's both a blessing and a curse at the same time, because on the upside, it's taken me Places, capital P.

On the downside, you always, without fail, crave for "something more".
It's even more fun if you can't identify what that thing is.

And of course, from the time perspective, all the memories of getting to that point fade away, so your brain easily gets fooled into thinking that it's so easy to make big changes. So just make another one.

Thanks, brain.
