I was thinking about Monogatari series the other day, because over the course of journaling I realized how some of the problems of the protagonists resonated with mine.

It feels nice to have something to be able to just scratch that itch, even if you cannot relate to their problems...

You kinda do... On a spiritual level.
I'm so aspirational of some of those stories. It might sound weird to say that I might be envious of a few characters, considering the amount of trauma they've been going through...

But above everything, I'm aspirational of them being able to stand back up and face the reality again.

Would I be able to do something like this?
