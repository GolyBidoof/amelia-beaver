Have you heard of the concept of hero projects?

The concept goes as follows: everyone wants to be remembered for something. They want to have achieved something.

Be it love, fame, winning a gold at the Olympics, the usual pretentious stuff.
I tried to have one hero project, and I personally think I did very well on that.

By all objective metrics, I've probably succeeded, but the hedonic treadmill didn't stop right at that point.

Who would've thought that there's a life after "and they lived happily ever after". Not me?
